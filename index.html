大模型安全遗忘课题研究

Safety unlearning for multimodal large language models

2026.01 – 2026.03
第二作者 / Second Author ACM MM 2026 审稿中 / Under Review CCF A

工作介绍 / Work Overview

该工作聚焦多模态大模型中的安全遗忘问题。现有方法常因施加过于平均的遗忘压力而带来过度拒答，或难以泛化到新的图文风险组合。研究将安全干预前移到图文表征融合的关键位置，根据样本中的可见证据估计风险强度，仅抑制与风险高度相关的表示方向，以兼顾安全性、可用性与跨场景泛化。

This work studies safety unlearning in multimodal large language models. Existing methods often apply overly uniform forgetting pressure, which can cause over-refusal, or fail to generalize to new image-text risk combinations. The study shifts safety intervention to the key stage where visual and textual representations are fused, estimates risk intensity from observable evidence in each sample, and suppresses only the directions most strongly associated with risk, aiming to balance safety, usability, and cross-scenario generalization.

大模型多轮拒答校准课题研究

Refusal calibration under benign multi-turn dialogue history

2026.01 – 2026.03
第一作者 / First Author ICIC 2026 审稿中 / Under Review CCF C

工作介绍 / Work Overview

该工作研究安全对齐模型在多轮对话中的过度拒答问题。模型在单轮场景下可能表现正常，但在前文出现良性审查、风险讨论等历史后，同一安全请求也可能被错误拒答。针对这一现象，研究通过构建多历史对比样本并与无历史情形进行锚定比较，校正由对话历史带来的额外保守偏移，在减少误拒的同时尽量保持对危险请求的拒答能力与跨历史一致性。

This work investigates over-refusal in safety-aligned models under multi-turn dialogue history. A model may behave normally in single-turn settings, yet incorrectly refuse the same safe request after benign history such as compliance review or risk discussion appears in the context. To address this issue, the study builds grouped samples across multiple benign histories and compares them against a no-history anchor, then calibrates the extra conservative bias introduced by dialogue history while preserving refusal on genuinely unsafe requests and improving cross-history consistency.

大模型安全红队测试课题研究

Budget-efficient red teaming for LLM safety evaluation

2025.11 – 2026.01
第三作者 / Third Author IJCAI 2026 审稿中 / Under Review 已通过第一轮审稿 / First-Round Review Passed CCF A(at submission)

工作介绍 / Work Overview

该工作面向大模型安全红队测试中的预算受限问题。许多方法更关注最终攻击成功率，却容易在有限查询下反复命中少数脆弱点，难以及早发现更多类型的安全缺陷。为此，研究将红队测试重新表述为预算约束下的失败发现问题，更强调早期发现效率与风险覆盖范围，并通过结合目标特征判断、历史经验利用和多样性导向的策略搜索，提高有限预算下的测试效率。

This work focuses on the budget-constrained setting of safety red teaming for large language models. Many existing methods emphasize final attack success rate, but under limited queries they often keep hitting a small number of weak points and struggle to discover broader categories of safety failures early. The study therefore reframes red teaming as a failure-discovery problem under budget constraints, with stronger emphasis on early discovery efficiency and risk coverage, and improves testing efficiency by combining target characterization, experience reuse, and diversity-oriented strategy search.

遥感视觉语言预训练课题研究

Remote sensing vision-language pre-training with hierarchical semantics

2025.12 – 2026.03
第四作者 / Fourth Author ACM MM 2026 审稿中 / Under Review CCF A

工作介绍 / Work Overview

该工作面向遥感视觉语言学习中的层次语义建模难题。由于遥感图像分辨率高、目标密集且尺度变化明显，仅依赖整图与全局文本对齐，往往难以同时刻画场景层级与局部细节。针对这一问题，研究构建了从全局场景到局部区域的多粒度监督方式，并同步增强视觉编码器对局部纹理和远距离空间关系的建模能力，以提升表示质量和跨数据集泛化表现。

This work targets the challenge of hierarchical semantic modeling in remote sensing vision-language learning. Because remote sensing images are high-resolution, densely structured, and contain large scale variation, relying only on whole-image and global-text alignment is often insufficient to capture both scene-level semantics and local details. To address this, the study builds a multi-granularity supervision scheme from global scenes to local regions, while also strengthening the visual encoder for local texture modeling and long-range spatial relationship modeling, thereby improving representation quality and cross-dataset generalization.

  • 绘制论文流程图，共同撰写论文。 Produced the workflow figure for the paper and contributed to the paper writing together with the team.
