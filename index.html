Research

Selected Research Projects

To avoid listing unpublished titles, the following projects are described by topic.

Selective safety unlearning for multimodal large language models

Second Author · 2026.01 – 2026.03

This project studies how to mitigate harmful multimodal behavior without triggering blanket refusal on benign inputs. The work focuses on connector-level intervention, sample-specific risk estimation, and utility-preserving suppression of risk-aligned directions.

  • Worked on experiment design, cloud-based training, figure production, and empirical analysis.
  • Focused on over-refusal, cross-modal risk, and out-of-distribution generalization.

Refusal calibration under benign multi-turn dialogue history

First Author · 2026.01 – 2026.03

This project investigates how benign yet compliance-heavy dialogue history can induce unnecessary refusals on safe requests. It explores lightweight calibration strategies that separate the original refusal boundary from history-driven conservative bias.

  • Implemented the main pipeline in Python and completed end-to-end analysis and writing.
  • Emphasized robustness and consistency across different conversational histories.

Budget-efficient red teaming for LLM safety evaluation

Third Author · 2025.11 – 2026.01

This work frames red teaming as a budget-constrained failure discovery problem. It combines cognitive profiling, search-based strategy selection, and iterative refinement to discover diverse policy violations earlier and with fewer queries.

  • Used Python and API-based evaluation across four large models.
  • Contributed to experiments, data analysis, and visualization.

Remote sensing vision-language pre-training with hierarchical semantics

Fourth Author · 2025.12 – 2026.03

This project explores how to bridge global scene understanding and local fine-grained semantics in remote sensing imagery through multi-granularity supervision, regional semantic mining, and lightweight visual adaptation.

  • Participated in experiment iteration and figure design.
  • Contributed to project writing and visual presentation of results.
